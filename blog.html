My First Traveling Blog - 1

Visited: Bangladesh University of Engineering Technology (BUET)

How to get there?

The BUET campus is in the heart of the capital of Dhaka. It has a compact campus with halls of residences within walking distances of the academic buildings. At present the campus occupies 76.85 acres(31.1 hectares) of land. The academic area is defined by Shahid Sharani, Bakshi Bazar Road and Asian Highway.

What to eat there?

Campus Canteen Food: Always a go-to for budget-friendly meals like khichuri and bhaji. Biryani/Pulao: A staple at many local restaurants and takeaways near the university. Haleem/Khichuri: Popular comfort food, especially in cooler months, found at various eateries. Street Food: Fuchka, Chotpoti, and Jhalmuri are quick, cheap student favorites.
